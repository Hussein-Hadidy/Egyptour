Accommodation

Find the Perfect Place to Stay in Egypt

Types of Accommodation

Egypt offers diverse lodging options for every budget and preference

Luxury Hotels

High-end 5-star hotels with world-class amenities, fine dining, and premium services.

Key Attractions:
  • Concierge service
  • Spa & wellness centers
  • Multiple restaurants
  • Swimming pools
  • Room service 24/7

Mid-Range Hotels

Comfortable 3-4 star hotels offering good value with essential amenities and good locations.

Key Attractions:
  • Air conditioning
  • Restaurant
  • Room service
  • Wifi
  • Professional staff

Budget Hotels

Affordable 1-2 star hotels perfect for travelers on a tight budget.

Key Attractions:
  • Basic rooms
  • Fan or AC
  • Common areas
  • Budget-friendly
  • Local experience

Hostels

Social accommodations ideal for backpackers and solo travelers looking to meet others.

Key Attractions:
  • Shared dormitories
  • Common kitchen
  • Social events
  • Very affordable
  • Traveler community

Resorts

All-inclusive beach resorts offering comprehensive vacation packages with activities.

Key Attractions:
  • All-inclusive meals
  • Beach access
  • Water sports
  • Entertainment
  • Family-friendly

Nile River Cruises

Luxurious cruises along the Nile between Luxor and Aswan, combining travel and accommodation.

Key Attractions:
  • Onboard dining
  • Sightseeing included
  • Cultural programs
  • Unique experience
  • Expert guides

Recommended Areas to Stay

Choose your location based on the attractions and experience you want

Downtown Cairo

Central location with access to museums, markets, and nightlife. Good for culture enthusiasts.

  • Egyptian Museum nearby
  • Tahrir Square
  • Theaters and restaurants
  • Great nightlife

Giza Plateau

Close to the Pyramids with many hotels catering to tourists visiting the monuments.

  • Pyramid views
  • Tourist services
  • Pyramid Light Show
  • Tour operators

Zamalek & Garden City

Upscale neighborhoods on Nile islands with boutique hotels and restaurants.

  • Luxury hotels
  • Fine dining
  • Nile views
  • Island atmosphere

Luxor City

Strategic location for exploring temples and archaeological sites on both banks of the Nile.

  • Temple proximity
  • River activities
  • Local atmosphere
  • Tour operators

Aswan Waterfront

Serene riverside area perfect for relaxation with felucca sailing opportunities.

  • Felucca boats
  • River views
  • Nubian market
  • Peaceful setting

Alexandria Corniche

Mediterranean beachfront with modern hotels and seafood restaurants.

  • Beach access
  • Sea breeze
  • Seafood dining
  • Historic sites nearby

Booking Tips

1 Book accommodations at least 2-4 weeks in advance, especially during peak season (Oct-Apr)
2 Check reviews on TripAdvisor and Booking.com from recent travelers
3 Verify breakfast inclusion and cancellation policies before booking
4 Contact hotels directly for better rates, especially for multi-night stays
5 Book tours through your hotel for convenience and competitive rates
6 Consider booking Nile cruises through reputable tour operators or travel agencies
7 Ask about airport pickup/transfer services when booking
